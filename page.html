Inherited Family Trauma

Unconsciously, we relive our mother’s anxiety. We repeat our father’s disappointments. We replicate the failed relationships of our parents or grandparents.

Just as we inherit our eye color and blood type, we also inherit the residue from traumatic events that have taken place in our family. While our physical traits are easily discernible, this emotional legacy is often hidden from us. Anxiety, fear, financial worries, depression, illness and unhappy relationships can all be forms of our unconscious inheritance.

Unresolved traumas, some going back two or three generations, can ensnare us in feelings and situations that don’t even belong to us. They can forge a blueprint for our life, and can even pass onto our children.

It doesn’t have to continue. It can end here. Start by answering these questions.

Mark Wolynn

About Mark

Mark is North America’s leader in Inherited Family Trauma and inventor of the Core Language Approach, a revolutionary approach to healing trauma. The approach he's developed is taught worldwide and is profoundly effective in finding and resolving issues quickly. He works with patients one-on-one and in group workshops to treat depression, anxiety, obsessive thoughts, fears, panic disorders, self-injury, chronic pain and persistent conditions. Often, Mark can resolve patients issues in only one session or workshop.
